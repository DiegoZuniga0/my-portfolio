About Me

I am currently a Senior at the University of Indianapolis studying Electrical Engineering. I am a proud member of IEEE.

During my time at UIndy, I’ve had the opportunity to gain hands-on experience with circuits, embedded systems, and coding. I’ve worked with tools and languages such as C++, Verilog, FPGA development, and MATLAB.

I’ve also collaborated with other engineers on semester-long projects that emphasized real-world problem solving, innovation, and teamwork.

I’m passionate about my field and dedicated to turning ideas into reality through technology, design, and engineering.
